Multi-Agent Programming Contest 2020

I joined GOAL-DTU when I started writing my bachelor thesis about Multi-Agent Systems. GOAL-DTU is composed of a professor and students of DTU. The goal of the contest is to stimulate research on the topic of multi-agent systems. Normally the contest is arranged during fall but due to a tough qualification round, it was postponed to March 2021. GOAL-DTU qualified as the first team in September 2020. The goal of my bachelor thesis was to improve three areas of the multi-agent system used to qualify for the contest. The three areas were movement, communication, and strategy.
